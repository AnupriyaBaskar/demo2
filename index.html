    Dr.S.M.Shiva Nagendra is presently working as Professor in Department of Civil Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai India. He has more than 19 years' research experience in the field of air quality monitoring, modelling, management, control and policy implications. He has published more than 75 research publications in refereed journals, one reference book, and more than 100 papers in conferences.
